Grenade¶

Usage¶

Lua

  Vector(-900, 185, 215),
  Rotator(0, 90, 90),
  "NanosWorld::SM_Grenade_G67",
  "NanosWorld::P_Explosion_Dirt",
  "NanosWorld::A_Explosion_Large"
)
